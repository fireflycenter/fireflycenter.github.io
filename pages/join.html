Pediatric Occupational Therapist

We have an opening for a part time Occupational Therapist. The position is for both school and clinic-based work. Duties include screens, evaluations, consultation and direct service provision. Must be available some afternoons for clinic-based work.

Experience with children needed, sensory integration experience and school based/IEP experience highly desirable. Mentoring available for exceptional candidates. OTR and CA license required. Prefer responsible, reliable, creative person with strong written/verbal communication skills. Schedule flexibility and creative problem solving abilities helpful.

If interested, reply to: melisa@fireflycenter.com, and include a cover letter, resume and a writing sample (anonymous progress report or evaluation).
